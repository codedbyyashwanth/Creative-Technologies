Frequently asked questions

What is the typical timeline for developing a website?

Clients often want to know how long it will take to complete their website. The answer can vary depending on the complexity of the project, but providing a general timeframe can help set expectations.

How much does web development cost?

Clients often inquire about the cost of web development. You can provide a range or explain that the price depends on factors like the size and features of the website.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support after the website is live?

Many clients want to know if your service includes post-launch support and maintenance. Explain the extent of support you offer, such as updates, security, and troubleshooting.

Can you help with SEO and online marketing for the website?

Clients may be interested in optimizing their website for search engines and online marketing. You can outline the SEO services you offer and how they can benefit their online presence.

What technologies and platforms do you work with?

Potential clients may want to know if you are experienced with specific technologies or content management systems (e.g., WordPress, Webflow) and whether you can work with their preferred platform.
